Chennai

Chennai, on the Bay of Bengal in eastern India, is the capital of the state of Tamil Nadu. The city is home to Fort St. George, built in 1644 and now a museum showcasing the city’s roots as a British military garrison and East India Company trading outpost, when it was called Madras. Religious sites include Kapaleeshwarar Temple, adorned with carved and painted gods, and St. Mary’s, a 17th-century Anglican church.

Chennai had the third-largest expatriate population in India, at 35,000 in 2009, 82,790 in 2011 and estimated at over 100,000 by 2016.[18][19] Tourism-guide publisher Lonely Planet named Chennai as one of the top ten cities in the world to visit in 2015.[20] Chennai is ranked as a beta-level city in the Global Cities Index,[21] and was ranked the best city in India by India Today in the 2014 annual Indian city survey.
